Human Rights

Human rights are rights inhere to all human beings,regardless of race, sex, nationality, ethnicity, language, religion any other status.Human rights include the right to life and liberty,freedom from slavery and torture,freedom of opinion and expression,the right to work and education and many more. Everyone is entitled to these rights without discrimination. Baskaran foundation will protect and promote human rights and fundamental freedoms of individuals or groups.

Home for Destitute Children

Baskaran foundation is a non-profit,non-government,voluntary organization committed to the care of children.The aim & objective of the foundation is to provide family home to the destitute,orphaned,neglected,socially handicapped,children by giving them a family,home and strong foundation for an independent and secure life.Our Organization has always responded to the call of needy children arising out of emergency-whether natural or man-made. The association would care for children,many of whom they found on the streets,providing a home and preparing them for lives of dutiful and meaningful work.They built a smaller facility due to foster care and also had on site,group of cottages with an adult supervisor,these cottages being a real home with living room,dining room and bedrooms.

Family Counselling Center

The Family Counselling Center is functioning in Baskaran Foundation, It aims to help and empower individuals by assisting them through the legal system where cases regarding divorce,maintenance and property dispute are handled. FCC facilitates favorable environment for women,men and children to improve their family and home life.The family counselling center accepts the individuals and families dealing with issues ranging from spousal problems,greater family,disharmony or parent and child tensions. Counselors work to find sustainable solutions that satisfy the mental and emotional needs of all individuals or family members involved in the counselling process. The FCC offers job placements for destitute women,offering housemaid jobs,tailoring jobs in export companies and office jobs. Women in distress are provided with temporary shelter when needed. Baskaran foundation also works in collaboration with the police matters concerning marital problems,dowry harassment and domestic violence.
